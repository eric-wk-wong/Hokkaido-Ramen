ORIGIN

Sakuta Azusagawa was born in Hokkaido, Japan, in 1987. After graduating from high school, he worked at his father's restaurant for 8 years and grew a passion for cooking. His family's specialty was traditional Japanese noodles including Ramen and Udon. After graduating from the University of Tokyo with a degree in International Studies, he attended a culinary school in the United States. With the support from his family, Sakuta opened his first restaurant in New York City.
